Schedule

Saturday: on the water for morning practice
Sunday: either on the water in the mornings or at CRC for easy cardio switching every other week

Attendance

Mandatory. Illness excused with note. Common illness inexcuseable.

Team Captains

Two captains will be selected by the squad to hold non-officer leadership positions within the squad. Coxswains will be exempt from these roles as this is already expected from them.

Varsity Criteria

Rowers will be expected to have a a maximum 2k time of 8:30.00 Anyone who cannot meet this standard by the end of the previous winter semester will be asked to continue as a coxswains for the future season.

Coxswains

Expectations of coxswains will be increased significantly. I expect them all to perform on the level of assistant coaches to myself. A coxswain bootcamp will be held prior to the first week of school.
